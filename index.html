ℹ️ About Resonance

Resonance selectively amplifies specific frequencies while attenuating others. Turn noise into music, water into chimes, or friction into singing bowls.

How It Works

  • Energy Ratio (40%): % of energy in resonant band
  • Spectral Flatness (30%): How "peak-y" the spectrum is
  • Q Factor (30%): Filter selectivity (higher = more resonant)

Tips

  • Try high Q values (50+) with noise sources
  • Combine feedback with filters for self-oscillation
  • Use LFO modulation for evolving textures
  • Adjust wet/dry mix to blend processed sound
