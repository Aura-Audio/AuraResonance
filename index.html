ℹ️ Advanced Resonance Methods

🔔 Modal Synthesis

Simulates the natural resonant modes of physical objects (bells, plates, bars) using parallel bandpass filters with inharmonic frequency relationships.

🗣️ Formant Filtering

Mimics the human vocal tract with 5 parallel resonant filters. Creates vowel sounds ("ah", "ee", "oo") from noise sources.

🎶 Resonator Bank

Multiple independent resonators with harmonic relationships. Like having an array of singing bowls tuned to specific intervals.

📈 Dynamic Sweep

Envelope follower tracks input dynamics and drives filter cutoff. Creates expressive, responsive resonance that reacts to your playing.

🎸 Karplus-Strong

Classic string synthesis algorithm. Noise burst excites a filtered delay feedback loop, creating realistic plucked string sounds.

✨ Granular Resonance

Multiple short modulated delays with filtering. Creates shimmering, cloud-like textures from any sound source.
